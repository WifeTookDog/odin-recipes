Stuffed Mushrooms (Inspired by Tiana)

Dish Description


Imitation crab, white and cheddar cheese, roasted in the oven with store bought egg's bendict sauce, a dish you won't forget!

Ingredients

  • Imitation Crab
  • White Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Egg's bendict sauce (store bought)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 6969F
  2. Pull out all mushroom stems
  3. Using a shredder, shred up all of your cheese
  4. Cut up all your crab into bite sized pieces
  5. Stuff mushrooms with crab, cheese, and dazzle the sauce ontop.
  6. Throw in over for 69 minutes, and BOOM. Enjoy!
