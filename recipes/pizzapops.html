Pizza POPS

Dish Description


Have you ever wanted a smaller portion pizza that can be made in a couple minutes? Look no further! Pizza pops bring the good taste of a pizza in under 3 minutes!!

Ingredients

  • Pizza pops (store bought)

Instructions

  1. Drive to grocery store
  2. Buy Pizza pops
  3. Drive home from store
  4. Remove from packaging
  5. Put two pizza pops in a microwaveable safe dish
  6. Start microwave for 90 seconds
  7. Flip, and run for another 60 seconds
  8. ENJOY!!!!
